IBM has announced two critical quantum computing breakthroughs: the IBM Quantum Nighthawk processor featuring 120 qubits with enhanced connectivity, and a strategic shift to 300mm wafer fabrication at the Albany NanoTech Complex. These developments accelerate IBM's quantum roadmap, targeting quantum advantage by 2026 and fault-tolerant quantum computing by 2029. The manufacturing innovation enables 10x complexity increases and parallel design exploration, representing a significant advancement in quantum hardware production capabilities.
